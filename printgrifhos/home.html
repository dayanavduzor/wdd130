In imprenta Grafhos we make your ideas come to life. We print on fabric, glass, plastic, metal, ceramics, wood and paper. We work with both small and large quantities. Bring your ideas and we will help you built your design; you can also bring your own already made. About the methods we use:

OFFSET machine printing. Also called lithography, is a method of mass-production printing in which individuals pages of paper are fed into the machine. The main advantage is the high and consistent quality of image.

SILK printing. Also known as silk screen printing or screen printing. This technique involves printing ink through stencils that are supported by a porous fabric mesh stretched across a frame called a screen. The benefits are a higher quality output than digital printing, more durable for prints that are needed over the long term, it can be used in a variety of different print materials including glass, wood, textiles, signs banners, electronics, ceramics and much more.

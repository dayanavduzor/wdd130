Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the website is to attract and inform individuals who want to purchase or that are curious about the printing services offered by the shop. The website is needed and will be used to spread the word about the quality of the products and also to present ideas of what materials are possible to print on. The experiences of the customers and also to display information of how to find the shop. Everything will be done in a way that is appealing to any who may come across with it. This will benefit the business to communicate the value of what the company offers to the public and to convey professionalism, build revenue and create a positive opinion among the customers.

Audience

The Kind of people that are more likely to visit the website are business owners looking for sale notes with or without folio, signs, calendars and grocery bags for publicity. Also, people who are looking to personalize items for special events like: weddings, sweet sixteen parties, quincea√±era parties, birthdays etc. And at last, the students who sometimes are looking to print a variety of school projects. We need to consider the different financial situations for all these groups of people and that our audience usually could access the site from a desktop, laptop or mobile devices. Having this in mind, we want to build a site that is inviting and appealing for these groups of people.
